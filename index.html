Holiday Meal Calendar

About

In 2018 there was serveral Winnipeg charities that had holiday meals taking place on the same day. We thought that if there was a way to coordinate these meals in a public way it might avoid this problem and give those in need a chance to have a few meals over the holidays. This is the idea behind Holiday Meal Calendar.
